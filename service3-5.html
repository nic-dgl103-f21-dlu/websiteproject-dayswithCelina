Tinting

Arabella Beauty uses tinting as a semi permanent brow hair colour tint. No matter your hair colour, eyebrow or eyelash tinting will enhance your natural shape by filling those empty patches within your brows. Enhancing your hair colour, usually, one to two shades darker than your natural shade. Tinting will darken and beautify your brow shape which will give you the appearance of fluffy and full brows.
