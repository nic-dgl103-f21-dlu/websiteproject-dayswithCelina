Lifts, Tints & Lamination

Tinting

Arabella Beauty uses tinting as a semi permanent brow hair colour tint. No matter your hair colour, eyebrow or eyelash tinting will enhance your natural shape by filling those empty patches within your brows. Enhancing your hair colour, usually, one to two shades darker than your natural shade. Tinting will darken and beautify your brow shape which will give you the appearance of fluffy and full brows.

Lash Lifts

Arabella Beauty loves doing lash lifts. It’s a great way to enhance your beautiful natural lashes with a lifting and curling technique. Lash Lifts are semi permanent curling method on your lashes which will make your eyes pop. It’s a great alternative for those looking for a more natural, and lower maintenance option than eyelash extensions.

Brow Lamination

Eyebrow lamination is a great option for those who like bold, fluffy brows and have little hair work with. Arabella Beauty is happy to offer brow lamination! It’s a great option for those who want a lifted and smooth brow look. Brow lamination is a treatment in which straightens out your brow hair in a uniformed fashion to create a bold brow look.
